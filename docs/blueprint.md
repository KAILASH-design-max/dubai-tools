# **App Name**: InvoiceCraft for Dubai Tools

## Core Features:

- Invoice Generation: Create invoices with fields for item description, quantity, rate, amount, tax, and total.
- Customer Database: Maintain a database of customer information for easy invoice creation.
- Tax Calculation: Automatically calculate taxes based on predefined rates.
- Total Calculation: Automatically calculate subtotals, tax amounts, and grand totals.
- Company Information: Display Dubai Tools' address, phone numbers, email, and GST/Reg. No. on each invoice.
- Invoice Numbering: Automatically generate and manage invoice numbers.
- Invoice Design Tool: Suggest changes to improve invoice layouts, information density, and overall design through AI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5) to convey professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F0F0F5), creating a clean and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) to highlight key interactive elements.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' for body text. A clean, versatile sans-serif for readability.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' for headers and titles. Pairing the more computer-feeling 'Space Grotesk' with the user-friendly PT Sans
- Use clear, simple icons to represent invoice actions and elements.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout, ensuring readability and ease of use. Prioritize essential information such as amounts and dates.
- Subtle animations to confirm actions like saving or sending an invoice.
